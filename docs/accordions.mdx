# Accordions

Accordions show and hide content behind a clickable title, keeping dense pages scannable. The chevron rotates to mirror the open state, and the open and close motion is animated.

## Variants

<img className="light-only" src="/components/accordion-inline-light.png" alt="Inline accordion" />

| Variant | Open | Closed |
| ------- | ---- | ------ |
| Card    | Yes  | Yes    |
| Inline  | Yes  | Yes    |

- **Card** - A bordered, rounded surface with generous padding. Use it when the accordion stands on its own.
- **Inline** - No border, radius, or side padding. Use it inside cards, lists, or other surfaces that already provide their own container.
